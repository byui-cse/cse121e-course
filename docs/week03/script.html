CSE 121e: Week 01

Getting Started

Follow This Script

Using your Erlang Learning Assistant,
  1. Enter this into the prompt. "What is are stateful and stateless processes and why would I use them?"
  2. Ask your learning assistant for an analogy of stateful and stateless processes.
  3. Briainstorm with your team about what stateless and stateful processes are.
  4. Enter this into your prompt, "We think a stateless process <put your description here >. Is that right?"
  5. Repeat step 2 until everyone on your team is confident in their ability to explain to someone who doesn't know about these things what stateless and stateful processes are.
  6. Enter this into the prompt. "Show me 5 simple Erlang stateless processes and how to use them."
    1. Copy each example and execute it.
    2. Brainstorm with your team about what the parts of an an Erlang stateless process are, what each part does or means, and how they are used.
  7. Enter this into the prompt. "We think an Erlang stateless process consists of < put your description here >. Is that right?"
  8. Until everyone on your team is confident in their understanding of the parts of Erlang functions, loop back to #6.
  9. Enter this into the prompt. "Show me 5 simple Erlang statefull processes and how to use them."
    1. Copy each example and execute it.
    2. Brainstorm with your team about what the parts of an an Erlang statefull process are, what each part does or means, how they are used, and how they differ from stateless processes.
  10. Enter this into the prompt. "We think an Erlang stateless process consists of < put your description here >. Is that right?"
  11. Until everyone on your team is confident in their understanding of the parts of Erlang statefull processes, loop back to #9.
