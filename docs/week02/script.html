Getting Started

Follow This Script

Using your Erlang Learning Assistant,
  1. Enter this into the prompt. "Show me 5 simple Erlang functions that have two clauses."
    1. Brainstorm with your team about what the parts of an an Erlang function are and what each part does or means.
  3. Enter this into the prompt. "We think an Erlang function consists of < put your description here >. Is that right?"
  4. Until everyone on your team is confident in their understanding of the parts of Erlang functions, loop back to #1.
  5. Enter this into the prompt. "What are 10 of the most commonly used Erlang built in functions?"
      6. For each of this functions,
    1. Enter into the prompt, "Give me 5 examples of the use of the < put the function name here > function"
    2. Execute each of the results you get back by pasting them in the Erlang shell.
    3. Brainstorm with your team what each of these Built In Functions (BIFs) does until everyone on your team is confident they understand what each does.
